I'm tired

  • Leonid Kovalenko

    Read more posts by this author.

Leonid Kovalenko

14 Aug 2020 • 1 min read

This is what you probably do not want to hear from me. But anyway, this is a text dump on the outskirts of life. I write whatever.

I feel tired, especially at the first half of the day. This can manifest in the lack of energy, no purpose, and all those kinds of things, they call a depression. This is true. I think it is a scourge of our time. I don't know, may be it is my own bubble, but some people told me they suffer from it. Practically most of the people I have a contact with have those kind of issues. And I think it is deep enough. You can admit it. But there are so many things today you can compensate with. I probably had it forever.
